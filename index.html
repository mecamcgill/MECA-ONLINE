We Are MECA

We aid fellow Koreans get accustomed to the vibrant culture of Canada
and we introduce fellow Canadians to the remarkable culture of Korea.

We Promote Education

We strive to keep our community informed and connected. Whether that be through online-guides or networking conferences, we promise to deliver reliable academic information and offer diverse networking oppertunties with McGill Alumnis, Professors, and local Professionals.

We Promote Culture

We host and celeberate events that are close to the hearts of Koreans. Our cultural events provides a nostalgic atmosphere for some and new experiences for others. We invite everyone, whether you are Canadian or Korean, McGillian or Concordian, to experience Korea, right here, right in Montreal.

We are an Association

We value transparency and philanthropy. We regularly donate a portion of our profits to imporant causes and strive to raise awareness in various issues in Korea. Further, we strive to keep our financial affairs as transparents as possible- financial reports of all our events are readily available upon request.
