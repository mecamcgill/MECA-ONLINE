We Are MECA

We aid fellow Koreans get accustomed to the vibrant culture of Canada
and we introduce fellow Canadians to the remarkable culture of Korea.

We Promote Education

We strive to keep our community informed and connected. Whether that be through online-guides or networking conferences, we promise to deliver reliable academic information and maintain a healthy amount of professional connections.

We Promote Culture

We host and celeberate events that are close to the hearts of Koreans. Our cultural events provides a comforting familarity for some and new experiences for others. We also strive to be aware of Cultural issues and donate our profits to charities.

We are an Association

With the common purpose of spreading Korean Culture, we welcome everyone from Canadians to Koreans or McGillians to Concordians. We also seek to be as transparent as possible to our members and SSMU, financial reports of our events are always available upon request.
