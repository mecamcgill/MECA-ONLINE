We Are MECA

We help fellow Koreans get accustomed to the vibrant culture of Canada
and we introduce fellow Canadians to the remarkable culture of Korea.

We Promote Education

We strive to keep our community informed and connected. Whether that be through online-guides or networking conferences, we promise to deliver reliable academic information and offer diverse networking oppertunties with McGill Alumnis, Professors, and Local Professionals.

We Promote Culture

We host and celeberate events that are close to the heart of Koreans. Our cultural events provides a nostalgic atmosphere for some and new experiences for others. We invite everyone- whether you are Canadian or Korean, McGillian or Concordian- to experience Korea, right here in Montreal.
