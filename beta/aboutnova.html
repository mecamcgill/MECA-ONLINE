We Are
MECA.

We are McGill Koreans' Educational & Cultural Association.
Let's foster a united Korean community within McGill and
spread our culture to those unfamiliar.

We promote education

We strive to keep our community informed and connected. Whether that be through online-guides or networking conferences, we promise to deliver reliable academic information and offer diverse networking opportunities with McGill Alumni, Professors, and Local Professionals.



We promote culture

We host and celebrate cultural events that are close to the heart of Koreans. Our cultural events provide a nostalgic atmosphere for some and new experiences for others. We invite everyone- whether you are Canadian or Korean, McGillian or Concordian- to experience Korea, right here in Montreal.
